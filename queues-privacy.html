Privacy Policy for Mika-Queues

Last updated: 12 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Mika-Queues Discord application ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. By adding the Bot to a server or interacting with it, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who We Are

Mika-Queues is a Discord application that manages visitor queues for community Animal Crossing: New Horizons islands. The Bot is operated by the Treasure Islands community ("the Operator"). For privacy-related questions, contact us using the details in Section 10.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot collects and stores only the information necessary to operate the queue system:

  • Discord User ID — a numeric identifier Discord assigns to your account. We use this to track your place in queues, your assigned roles, and any warnings or restrictions.
  • Discord Server (Guild) ID — to keep each server's queue data separate.
  • In-game registration details — the in-game character name and island/town name you voluntarily provide when registering, so staff and other users can identify arrivals.
  • Queue activity — which queues you join or leave, your position, timestamps, and visit counts.
  • Moderation records — warnings issued to you (with timestamps and reasons), and any temporary or permanent blacklist status, used to enforce community rules.

The Bot does not read or store the content of your messages, your online/presence status, your email address, your IP address, or any payment information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Place you in and move you through island visit queues.
  • Assign and remove temporary roles that grant access to island code channels.
  • Enforce time limits, fair-use rules, warnings, and blacklists.
  • Display arrival information to community staff and members.
  • Maintain visit statistics for the community.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating the queue system.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable (for example under the UK GDPR / EU GDPR), we process your information on the basis of your consent (given by choosing to use the Bot) and our legitimate interest in operating a functional, fair queue system for the community.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We do not share it with third parties except:

  • Discord, as necessary for the Bot to function on the platform (subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy).
  • Where required by law or to protect the safety and rights of the community.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored in a private database accessible only to the Operator. We take reasonable measures to protect it from unauthorised access. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, but we limit access strictly to what is needed to run the Bot.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as needed to operate the queue system. Specifically:

  • Warnings automatically expire and are deleted after a period of good standing.
  • Queue activity is transient and overwritten as queues update.
  • Registration and visit data are kept while you remain an active community member.

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Withdraw consent and stop using the Bot.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using Section 10. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at anyone under the minimum age required to use Discord in their country (at least 13). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone below that age.

10. Contact

For any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, contact the Operator through the Treasure Islands community server, or via the contact method listed on our community page.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the community server, and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
