Privacy Policy

Treasure Islands Subscription Bot (Mika-Bot) · Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Treasure Islands subscription bot ("the bot", "we") collects, why, and how it is used. By subscribing through the bot, you agree to the practices described here.

What We Collect

  • Discord User ID — to identify you, assign and remove your subscriber role, and link you to your subscription.
  • Subscription details — your plan, status, start and renewal dates, and cancellation reason (if you choose to provide one).
  • Payment references — a customer/subscription identifier and the email address associated with your Stripe or PayPal payment. We use this to match payments to your account and handle billing issues.

We do not collect, store, or read your Discord messages, and we do not track your online presence or activity.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely by Stripe and PayPal. We never see or store your full card number, bank details, or payment credentials. Those are handled entirely by the payment providers under their own privacy policies (Stripe, PayPal).

How We Use Your Data

  • To grant and remove subscriber roles based on your payment status.
  • To run a periodic sync that keeps your roles accurate.
  • To respond to billing questions and support requests.
  • To send you subscription-related direct messages (e.g. a welcome message).

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except the payment providers (Stripe, PayPal) necessary to process your subscription. We may disclose data if required by law.

Data Retention

We keep your subscription data for as long as you have an active or recently lapsed subscription, and as needed for billing records and dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your data (see below), subject to any records we are legally required to retain.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by opening a billing ticket in the Treasure Islands Discord server. Cancelling your subscription stops further data collection.

Contact

For any questions about this policy or your data, please open a billing ticket in the Treasure Islands Discord server.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
